# Why Outline?

Are you passionate about digital freedom and helping users bypass censorship?
Outline is the solution you need.

- **Expand Access to Information:** Help your users to overcome censorship
  barriers and access the open internet, no matter where they are.

- **Prioritize Privacy and Security:** Protect your users' online activities
  with Outline's robust encryption, ensuring their internet traffic data remains
  confidential and secure when it's traveling through their local or national
  network.

- **Simplify Setup for Everyone:** Outline's user-friendly design empowers your
  users to set up and manage their VPN connections effortlessly, even without
  technical expertise.

- **Build Trust with Transparency:** Outline's open-source foundation
  demonstrates your commitment to transparency and fosters trust with your
  users.

Join the movement for a truly open internet. With Outline, you can provide a
lifeline to users in restricted regions, enabling them to experience the web
without limits and reclaim their digital freedom.
